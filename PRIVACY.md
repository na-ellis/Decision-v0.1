# Privacy Notice

**Effective Date:** March 2026

Decision Profiler is designed as a local-first tool. This notice explains what data the app stores, where it is stored, and what privacy risks users should understand before using it.

---

## 1. How the app stores data

Decision Profiler stores app data locally in your browser using browser storage on the device and browser profile you are using. In the current version, the app is intended to operate without requiring a user account or a remote application database for normal use.

---

## 2. What data may be stored

The app may store information that you enter or create, including:

- decision titles, descriptions, and notes
- profile records and behavioral observations
- analysis inputs and saved outputs
- templates, settings, and related app configuration
- imported or exported app data files

This information may include personal, professional, strategic, or otherwise sensitive content depending on how you use the tool.

---

## 3. No analytics or tracking in this version

This version of Decision Profiler does not use advertising trackers or analytics tools to monitor your activity inside the app. It is also not designed to require an account for basic use.

---

## 4. No routine transmission of your app content

In the current version, your profile and decision content is intended to remain on your device during normal use rather than being transmitted to a server by the application itself.

---

## 5. Important local privacy risks

Even when data stays on your device, privacy risks still exist. For example:

- anyone with access to the same device and browser profile may be able to access stored app data
- exported backup files may contain your stored data in readable form
- browser storage may persist until manually deleted
- device compromise, shared access, or unmanaged backups may expose stored content

Users should avoid storing highly regulated, classified, or otherwise restricted information in the app unless they have separately validated that doing so is appropriate for their environment.

---

## 6. Export, import, and deletion

The app allows users to export stored data, import data, and delete locally stored data.

Please note:

- exported files should be stored and transferred securely
- deleting data from the app does not delete copies of exported files saved elsewhere
- imported files should only come from trusted sources

---

## 7. Shared devices

If you use Decision Profiler on a shared, public, borrowed, or managed device, your locally stored data may be visible to other users of the same browser profile unless it is removed.

---

## 8. Your responsibility for entered content

You control what information you enter into the app. If you enter names, roles, performance-related observations, internal strategy, or sensitive decision material, you are responsible for ensuring that your use of the tool aligns with your organization's privacy, HR, legal, records, and security requirements.

---

## 9. Changes to this notice

This Privacy Notice may be updated as the application changes. The version made available with the app should be treated as the current notice for that release.
